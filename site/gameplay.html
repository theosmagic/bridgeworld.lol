Game Overview

Bridgeworld is a strategy game of resource management, exploration, and coordination. Players control Legions to quest, craft, and harvest MAGIC from Harvesters.

⚔
Legions
Your characters in Bridgeworld. Train them, send them on quests, and stake them in Harvesters.
💎
Treasures
Collectible items with various rarities. Used for crafting and boosting Harvesters.
🔥
Crafting
Use the Forge to create consumables and treasures from gathered materials.
🌟
Harvesters
Colossal creatures that produce MAGIC. Stake permits and MAGIC to earn rewards.

Harvesters

Harvesters are colossal creatures from Bridgeworld that bestow MAGIC rewards to those who earn their favor. Players compete in games to gain access and stake MAGIC in Harvesters.

Key Mechanic: Harvesters compete against each other in producing MAGIC. The better fed and maintained they are, the more MAGIC they pull away from other Harvesters.
